# Dataframe with palaeoclimatic data.

A dataframe containing palaeoclimate data at 1 ky temporal resolution
with the following columns:

## Usage

``` r
data(climate)
```

## Format

dataframe with 6 columns and 800 rows.

## Details

- *age* in kiloyears before present (ky BP).

- *temperatureAverage* average annual temperature in degrees Celsius.

- *rainfallAverage* average annual precipitation in millimetres per day
  (mm/day).

- *temperatureWarmestMonth* average temperature of the warmest month, in
  degrees Celsius.

- *temperatureColdestMonth* average temperature of the coldest month, in
  degrees Celsius.

- *oxigenIsotope* delta O18, global ratio of stable isotopes in the sea
  floor, see
  [http://lorraine-lisiecki.com/stack.html](http://lorraine-lisiecki.com/stack.md)
  for further details.
